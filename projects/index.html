CarNet

A system that enables cars to broadcast their intended actions, helping nearby vehicles respond proactively and reduce the risk of accidents.

Driving is dangerous, but it doesn't have to be.

This is the first step I'm taking to unlock that future.

Pocket Prosthetic

An IOS app that uses an iPhone's LiDAR sensor to create 3D scans of a user's residual limb which they can then send to their prosthetist.

This allows anyone, anywhere to create and access high-quality custom prosthetics.
